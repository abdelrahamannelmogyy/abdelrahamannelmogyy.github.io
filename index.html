About Me

I'm a Data Analyst and Automation Engineer based in Cairo, Egypt, with 2+ years of experience turning messy operational data into clear decisions — and then automating the work so it never has to be done manually again.

My focus sits at the intersection of business intelligence and intelligent automation. At Maharah International I built the reporting infrastructure from scratch — Power BI dashboards connected live to SQL Server, Python scripts that replaced hours of daily manual work, and a VAPI voice agent system integrated directly with the company's database for automated debt collection calls. That combination of BI + automation + voice AI is what makes my profile uncommon.

Outside my full-time role I work as a freelance Python developer, building automation tools for clients across different industries. I take on projects where the problem is real and the solution needs to be reliable.

I'm actively looking for opportunities to work with international teams — whether remotely or with relocation to Europe, the Gulf, or Canada — where data work actually moves the business forward.
